### briantwai (1960) vs flopflop (2116) | `wO2Oh-yyz0I`

**Set:** Resophore, Synthesizer, Cryo Ray, Chrono Filter, Feral Warden, Doomed Mech, Tyranno Smorcus, Militia, Galvani Drone

This set is a chill nightmare. **Tyranno Smorcus** (frontline, 2 attack + 1 chill per turn) and **Cryo Ray** (permanent 1 chill) stack into granularity denial that absorbers cannot answer. **Galvani Drone** enables cheap, renewable energy for **Forcefield** spam. Feral Warden and lifespan units offer traditional defence, but they crumble under layered chill. Whoever commits to chill attack early forces the opponent into permanent react mode. Whoever builds absorbers without chill mitigation loses.

briantwai reads this fast. flopflop does not.

10 turns. Chill wins.
