## "Protoplasm Is King"
### Wonderboat vs Homeless | `WjhmP-WWdXx`
**Ranked | 45+45/45 | 16 turns | Wonderboat (2298) wins over Homeless (2228)**

**Set:** Protoplasm, Bloodrager, Oxide Mixer, Shiver Yeti, Lucina Spinos, Chrono Filter, Blood Phage, Cluster Bolt, Asteri Cannon

This set offers two visions of red. **Path A: The Red Grind** -- Blood Phage (self-funding 1g+1atk/turn), Tarsier, Bloodrager. Slow-burn engine that compounds. **Path B: The Green Burst** -- Protoplasm (prompt 4HP blocker OR click for 4 burst attack, self-destructs), paired with Cluster Bolt (4G for 4 Gauss Charges). Requires dual-tech (Conduit + Animus), but delivers focused damage that overwhelms steady defence.

The expensive legendaries are pipedreams in a game this fast. The real decision: grind or burst?

== MESSAGE 2 ==

### The Fork (T1-T8)

Standard DD openings. Then **Turn 4**: Homeless spends 33.6s thinking, buys **Drone + Animus**. Early red -- signalling Blood Phage/Tarsier pressure.

Wonderboat responds T5: **Conduit.** 2.7 seconds. Green, not red. The Protoplasm plan is locked in.

T6 confirms both paths. Homeless: **Blood Phage + Tarsier**. Wonderboat T7, in 3.0s flat: **Conduit, Animus, Drone, Conduit**. Three Conduits and an Animus -- dual-tech aimed at Protoplasm. Every Wonderboat turn from T3 to T9 takes under 5 seconds. The game plan was decided during Homeless's 33-second think. Everything after is execution.

Homeless T8: **Blastforge + Rhino**. Preparing for steady red aggression. Not Protoplasm burst.

== MESSAGE 3 ==

### The Siege (T9-T16)

**T9: First Protoplasm.** The weapon is online. Prompt -- immediately a 4HP blocker or 4 burst damage.

T10: Homeless buys **Wall + Bloodrager**. Full red engine assembling. But there's already a defence phase -- Wonderboat's attack is arriving.

**T11: 26 clicks in 4.5 seconds.** Wonderboat fires off Protoplasm, Cluster Bolts, Shiver Yetis, Forcefields -- testing every purchase at speed, buying what resources allow. The *intent* was a devastating alpha strike. The *reality* was still potent: fresh Protoplasm burst plus Forcefields (sacrificing Drones for emergency defence) and more Conduits for future green.

**T12: Homeless thinks for 118 seconds.** Two full minutes. 23 clicks, almost all blocker assignments. Every available unit thrown into defence. Buys: Wall, Shiver Yeti, Engineer. Purely survival.

== MESSAGE 4 ==

The asymmetry is fatal. Wonderboat attacks with *disposable* burst -- Protoplasm self-destructs, Gauss Charges auto-fire and vanish. Homeless defends with permanent units that get chewed up. Every Drone blocking stops producing gold. The defence works each turn, but the economy behind it crumbles.

T13-14: Wonderboat buys another Protoplasm (bank crashes from 77s to 4s -- the only slow think). Homeless buys 1 Shiver Yeti, spends the entire turn assigning 10 blockers. T15: Wonderboat delivers **Protoplasm + Cluster Bolt** -- the wave that breaks it. T16: Homeless buys 1 Wall. GG.

**What decided it?** Set reading. Both Master-tier veterans (Homeless has 31,884 rated games). Wonderboat identified Protoplasm as dominant within 30 seconds and never deviated. Homeless played the standard red aggression game -- and played it well. But burst beats grind in this set. Protoplasm deals 4 damage *now*. Blood Phage deals 1 *starting two turns later*.

16 turns. 5:55. Protoplasm is king.
