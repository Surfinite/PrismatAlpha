# Feeding the Beast | FxCfR-K49T+
**Surfinite (1856) vs Kolento (2222)** — Result: Surfinite wins in 15 turns

**Set:** Animus, Vai Mauronax, Plasmafier, Ebb Turbine, Forcefield

An upset between a 1856 and a 2222. The set offers two expensive finishers — Plasmafier (12GGGB, eats Drones for 4 damage each) and Vai Mauronax (13BCCCC, freezes defenders with Chill 7) — but neither player goes for Vai, and only one goes for Plasmafier. Ebb Turbine is also ignored. The game comes down to Plasmafier vs raw red aggression.

Both players open with Thorium Dynamo for energy income. Kolento saves gold through T3 while Surfinite picks up a Galvani Drone and a second Thorium Dynamo. On T4 Surfinite commits to double Blastforge — the blue infrastructure needed for Plasmafier and Tesla Coil.

Kolento dumps his gold reserve on T5, picking up Blastforges and Animus — loading up on both blue and red resource production. The intent is clear: Tarsiers and Rhinos are about to flood the board.

Surfinite answers on T6 with **Plasmafier** — the most expensive unit purchased all game. The eval immediately favours Surfinite (59%) and stays there. Kolento starts building his red army with Tarsiers and Feral Wardens, backed by Mahar Rectifier for pseudo-absorb.

From T7 onward it's a race: can Kolento's attackers break through before the Plasmafier eats enough Drones to win? Surfinite builds Tesla Coil (T7), then stacks Walls, Galvani Drones, and Engineers. A second Tesla Coil follows by T9 with Forcefields buying time. Kolento sends Feral Wardens every turn — prompt blockers that also chip in attack — backed by Rhinos and a Gauss Cannon.

The eval is brutal for Kolento. He drops to 31% by T7 and keeps sliding — 27% at T11, 21% soon after. He adapts, adding Steelsplitters and a Conduit for green, but the defensive wall is too deep. Surfinite picks up a second Mahar Rectifier on T10 for rotating pseudo-absorb, then starts adding Steelsplitters and Feral Wardens of his own — persistent attackers that don't require Drone sacrifice.

By T12-13 Kolento is buying Engineers and Feral Wardens each turn, trying to stabilise. The Feral Wardens serve double duty — prompt blocking plus attack — but each one only absorbs 3 damage once (Fragile). Against Plasmafier bursts, that's an expensive trade.

Kolento's final turns tell the story. He scrambles for both defense and damage — Walls, Forcefields, Rhinos, Engineers — but the eval reads 36% and falling.

Surfinite closes it out at 73% eval with Forcefields and Walls. The fortress holds, the Plasmafier has done its work.

**Why it worked:** Plasmafier needs two things — a Drone pipeline and a wall strong enough to survive while your economy shrinks. Tesla Coil + Mahar Rectifier + Walls + Forcefields gave Surfinite exactly that. Kolento's wide red army (Feral Wardens, Tarsiers, Rhinos) generates steady pressure, but without a big absorber to anchor defense, the position collapses once the Plasmafier reaches critical mass. `FxCfR-K49T+`
