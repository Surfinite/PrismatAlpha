# Feeding the Beast | FxCfR-K49T+
**Surfinite (1856) vs Kolento (2222)** — 15 turns

A 366-point rating upset in a set defined by one unit: **Plasmafier**. Kolento — the legendary card game competitor — brought double Animus aggression with Feral Wardens and Rhinos. Surfinite answered with a Drone-eating monster behind a fortress of Walls and Tesla Coils.

**Set:** Animus, Vai Mauronax, Plasmafier, Ebb Turbine, Forcefield
Neither player touched Vai Mauronax or Ebb Turbine.

**Opening (T1-3):** Mirror economy. Both pick up Thorium Dynamo for energy income, standard Drone buys. Nothing unusual — both players feeling out the set.

**The Fork (T4-6)**

Here the game splits wide open. Surfinite buys double Blastforge on T4 — heavy blue that signals big tech. Turn 5 brings a Wall and Mahar Rectifier, the regenerating blocker-attacker that pseudo-absorbs 4 damage across turns.

Kolento's T5 is a statement: double Blastforge, double Animus, Thorium Dynamo, and Drones — *six purchases*. Four red sources means Tarsiers and Rhinos are coming in waves.

Then T6 — the game-defining moment. Surfinite drops **Plasmafier** (12GGGB). This unit deals 4 base attack *plus* 4 more for every Drone it consumes. It's an economy-to-damage conversion engine that demands constant Drone production to keep firing. Kolento goes Mahar Rectifier + Tarsier, beginning the red aggression — but the beast is already online.

**The Arms Race (T7-12)**

Surfinite builds layered defense: Tesla Coil on T7 (5HP absorber), then floods the board with Walls, Engineers, Galvani Drones, and Forcefields. A second Tesla Coil arrives T9. The plan: build an impenetrable wall, keep producing Drones, and feed them to the Plasmafier.

Kolento's counter is relentless width. Feral Wardens flow in every turn from T7 — prompt 3HP blockers that double as attackers. Combined with Rhinos and Tarsiers, Kolento builds a red army generating serious pressure. It's a classic strategy: overwhelm with efficient attackers.

But the Plasmafier scales differently. Surfinite keeps the Drone pipeline running through Conduits, adding a second Mahar Rectifier on T10 for rotating pseudo-absorb. By T11, 16 ability activations — the engine is fully operational, consuming Drones for massive burst while the Tesla Coil fortress soaks incoming damage.

**The Collapse (T13-15)**

Kolento keeps buying Feral Wardens and Rhinos, but the math has turned. Surfinite's Steelsplitters (T11, T13, T14) add persistent attack without Drone sacrifice, and a Gauss Cannon on T15 seals it.

The tell is Kolento's final turn: **64 clicks and 3 reverts** — desperately searching for a defensive configuration that holds. Multiple Walls, Rhinos, 4 Forcefields... it's not enough. The Plasmafier burst combined with Steelsplitters punches through.

**Surfinite takes it in 15 turns.**

Plasmafier punishes wide red armies. Kolento's Feral Wardens and Tarsiers die efficiently to 4-damage bursts, and without a large absorber to anchor defense, the position crumbles at breach. Vai Mauronax might have helped here — ironically unused by either side.

Build the engine. Build the fortress. Feed the beast. `FxCfR-K49T+`
