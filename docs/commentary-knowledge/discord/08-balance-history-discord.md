# Balance Opinion (Discord)

Extracted from Prismata Discord (52 insights).

### Amporilla has many counters and won't win most sets, but completely dominates and outscales everything when it does win, making it a high-variance unit that feels oppressive ('salt unit') without being fundamentally overpowered.

> Source: Discord #unknown -- apooche (2018-01-16)

**Confidence:** high | **Temporal:** timeless
