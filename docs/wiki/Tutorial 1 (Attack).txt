= Tutorial 1 (Attack) =

'''Tutorial 1 (Attack)''' is the first level in the Prismata campaign.

==Setup==

The player begins with 3 [[Steelsplitter]]s and 1 [[Omega Splitter]]. The opponent begins with 3 [[Wall]]s and 5 [[Tarsier]]s.

==Solution==

Because the NPC possesses only 5 total {{ticon|attack}}, it is impossible for him to destroy your [[Omega Splitter]] and you therefore cannot lose. A sample solution looks like:

# {{ticon|click}} 3 [[Steelsplitter]]s
# {{ticon|blocker}} with [[Omega Splitter]], {{ticon|click}} 3 [[Steelsplitter]]s
# {{ticon|blocker}} with [[Omega Splitter]], {{ticon|click}} 3 [[Steelsplitter]]s
# {{ticon|blocker}} with [[Omega Splitter]], {{ticon|click}} 3 [[Steelsplitter]]s
# {{ticon|blocker}} with [[Omega Splitter]], {{ticon|click}} 3 [[Steelsplitter]]s
