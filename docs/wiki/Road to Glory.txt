= Road to Glory =

Missions:

# Complete chapter 1 of the campaign.
# Clear 5 Beginner Scenarios in Combat Scenario mode.
# Defeat Basic Bot honorably.
# Clear 5 Intermediate Scenarios in Combat Scenario mode.
# Win a Casual Match game.
# Defeat Adept Bot honorably.
# Obtain 20 different badges by completing achievements.
# Clear 5 Advanced Scenarios in Combat Scenario mode.
# Reach level 20.
# Defeat Expert Bot honorably.
# Clear 5 Expert Scenarios in Combat Scenario mode.
# Obtain 10 victories in Casual Match mode.
# Clear 5 of the Concept Pack puzzles.
# Clear an Expert Challenge in the campaign.
# Defeat Master Bot honorably.
# Reach level 30.
# Win a game in Ranked Play mode.
# Beat 5 Master Scenarios in Combat Scenario mode.
# Obtain 40 different badges by completing achievements.
# Reach Tier 4 in Ranked Play.
# Obtain 4 expert medals and clear the chapter 1 bonus mission.
# Reach level 40.
# Reach Tier 6 in Ranked Play mode.
# Earn 15 points in a "Mode of the Week" or "Potpourri" event.
# Reach Tier 7 in Ranked Play mode.
# Obtain the "100 honorable victories" achievement.
# Obtain 60 different badges by completing achievements.
# Reach Tier 8 in Ranked Play mode.
# Earn 15 points in a "Blitz Brawl" event.
# Reach Tier 9 in Ranked Play mode.
# Earn 9 Expert Medals in the campaign.
# Obtain 80 different badges by completing achievements.
# Reach Tier X in Ranked Play mode.

Definition of honorable victory: Win a Ranked Play, Casual Match, or Master Bot game (with 5+ all random units without any handicaps).
