==Strategy==
The '''{{PAGENAME}}''' grows a crystalline [[Resource]] called [[Green resource|Gaussite]], more commonly referred to as {{ticon|green}}[[green]]. Green is similar to {{ticon|gold}}[[Gold]], as it can be stored between turns. 

Due to its low cost, a Conduit is easy to fit into builds, especially for player 2. The Conduit is also the least committal tech building, because Green can be stored up and costs the least Gold to spend. Because it is cheap and noncommittal, a Conduit is often the first tech building players will purchase. That said, remember that storing up lots of Green that you are unable to spend can be just as bad as letting {{ticon|red}}[[Red resource|Red]] or {{ticon|blue}}[[Blue resource|Blue]] decay, so even Conduits should be built sparingly.

In games featuring high cost [[green unit]]s, it can be good to purchase a Conduit early so that you can buy the high cost unit without having to make too many Conduits. For example, players often buy [[Xaetron]] with only two Conduits.
