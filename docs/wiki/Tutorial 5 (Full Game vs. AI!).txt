'''Tutorial 5 (Full Game vs. AI!)''' is the fifth level in the Prismata campaign.

==Setup==

The setup is that of a Base+5 game. The player begins with 2 [[Engineer]]s and 6 [[Drone]]s while the opponent begins with 2 [[Engineer]]s and 7 [[Drone]]s.

The five expansion cards are always the same: namely, [[Gauss Fabricator]], [[Flame Animus]], [[Husk]], [[Amporilla]], and [[Centurion]].
