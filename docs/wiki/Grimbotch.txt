==Strategy==
'''{{PAGENAME}}''' is a cheap offensive [[red unit]] with a limited {{ticon|lifespan}}4 [[lifespan]]. It can be thought of as a [[Rhino]] without {{ticon|prompt}}[[Prompt]] but costing 1{{ticon|gold}}[[gold]] less and able to attack one more time.

The main use of {{PAGENAME}} is as a temporary attacker for the mid-to-late game, similar to how you might use [[Rhino]]s but ''much'' better. They are extremely efficient at this role, enough to often justify [[Animus]] in sets where you wouldn't otherwise want it. However, be aware that in most cases you should not buy {{PAGENAME}}s as your first attackers, becuse they will soon expire and your damage will stop increasing while you begin to fall behind in constant damage. Thus you would be better off starting with permanent attackers (such as [[Tarsier]]s) and transitioning to Grimbotches only when you have a respectable amount of permanent attack. The exception is when the opponent has only an [[Animus]] and few [[Drone]]s with which to buy defense, as it will only take a small amount to damage to overcome them. For this reason it is often foolish to miror a very early Animus in {{PAGENAME}} sets.

As with all units with {{ticon|lifespan}}[[lifespan]] and {{ticon|blocker}}[[blocker]], {{PAGENAME}} is most efficient when used to take fatal damage on the turn that it would die. Mind the [[lifespan blocker counters|availability of counters]] to this strategy however. In a nutshell it is more useful as {{ticon|health|2}} on its last turn, but you might consider {{ticon|attack}}attacking with it in cases when getting the [[absorb]] in defense is uncertain.
