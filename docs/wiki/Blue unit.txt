= Blue unit =

'''{{PAGENAME}}s''' are those that cost only {{ticon|blue}}[[blue]] [[technology]].

Blue units tend to have higher health than [[red unit]]s, while not as high as that of [[green unit]]s. Unlike green units, however, they are not fragile. Blue units therefore make the most efficient blockers, especially when it comes to absorb.

The most expensive blue units in Prismata require {{ticon|blue}}3. This means having 3 [[Blastforge]]s in play, or some alternative such as a [[Synthesizer]]. A lot of these expensive blue units are also limited in supply, so consider how you will transition from playing these expensive units into an army (typically [[Steelsplitter]]s are the way to go).
