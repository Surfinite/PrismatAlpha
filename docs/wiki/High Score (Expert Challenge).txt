= High Score (Expert Challenge) =

__NOTOC__ <!--suppresses table of contents for the headers!-->

High Score is the bonus mission and last (9th) mission of the [[Episode 2|second episode]] in the [[Campaign|Campaign]]. This mission is extremely tough and stumps even the most veteran of Prismata players. <br/>

<br/><div class="contentbox mw-contentbox ">
::'''<big>Victory window message</big>'''
::'' It's actually possible to clear this Expert Challenge without buying Ossified Drone at all (but only barely!)'' </div>



=='''Hints and solution'''==

===General hints and tips===

{|   class="wikitable mw-collapsible mw-collapsed"
| <strong>+ Show hints&nbsp;</strong>
|-
| Since the amount of [[Rhino|Rhino]] [[Stamina|stamina]] is limited and the objective is to survive until Turn 30, you will need to kill [[Gunbot Forge]] eventually.
|-
| As the victory window message suggests: [[Ossified Drone|Ossified Drone]] is rather inefficient and you should delay getting it until as late as possible.
|-
| It is better value to kill [[Tough Gunbot]] compared to [[Gunbot Forge]]. The former being a 3 [[Health|health]] [[Unit|unit]] that produce 1 [[Attack|attack]]/turn compared to a 4 [[Health|health]] [[Unit|unit]] that produce 1 [[Health|health]]/turn (assuming that the [[Gunbot|Gunbot]] spawned by [[Gunbot Forge]] gets killed immediately). 
|-
| It is possible to win killing 1 [[Tough Gunbot]] or 2 [[Tough Gunbot]].
|}

{|   class="wikitable mw-collapsible mw-collapsed"
| <strong>+ Show extra hints&nbsp;</strong>
|-
| It is possible to have 26 Drones on the field at once.
|-
| This puzzle has some small leeway, because your opponent must kill your last few Animus as well.
|-
| You want to get in a kind of sustaining cycle in which you can take all of your opponent's attack and kill a Gunbot each turn.
|}

===Solution===

This is the best known solution for Expert High Score. Defined by highest turn number survived followed by least damage overkilled(by the bot). <br/>
Annotations: Before ")" means [[Absorb|absorb]] on (ctrl-click); after "(" means hold back. Otherwise always assume to [[Absorb|absorb]] on the highest [[Health|health]] [[Blocker|blocker]] and click every unit. 1=[[Ossified Drone]], 2=[[Ferritin Sac|Ferritin Sac]]
