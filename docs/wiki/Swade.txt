= Swade =

{{stub}}

[[File:Swade.png|150px|right]]
'''Swade''' is a character in the campaign of [[Prismata]]. He is a swarmwielder and serves as the playable character in all current campaign missions. He used to work with the special forces and frequents a bar with an unknown name.<ref>http://blog.lunarchstudios.com/2014/07/08/prismata-game-art-setting-the-bar-with-mike-fong/</ref>
[[File:Prismata swade wong.png|thumb|Swade Wong info page]]

==Trivia==
The name Swade was made out of the first letter in the names of the first five people involved with Prismata.<ref>http://www.reddit.com/r/Prismata/comments/2phqkr/cbc_news_article_on_prismata_the_game_is_headed/cmxm5nm?context=1</ref>
