Mike Fong is the in-house writer for Lunarch Studios. During the kickstarter campaign for update 2014-05-12 Fong produced [https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=yFqlEO59_6s a video detailing some of the basics of Prismata's setting and plot].

== Setting ==

The setting for Prismata is Beacon One, a tide-locked planet that is otherwise similar to Earth with plant and animal life.
The light side is governed by The Alliance, a human organization. The dark side is home to a faction of robots which defected from their human masters.

== Plot ==

The plot for the game will follow the story of a Swarm Wielder named [[Swade]] as he attempts to deal with a plague known as V.I.L.E, which is making robots more aggressive and defiant.<ref>https://www.kickstarter.com/projects/lunarchstudios/prismata-a-new-hybrid-game-of-pure-strategy</ref>
