= The Mines (Expert Challenge) =

__NOTOC__ <!--suppresses table of contents for the headers!-->

The Mines is the 3rd mission of the [[Episode 3|third episode]] in the [[Campaign|Campaign]]. The mission forces the player to manage offense, defense with low absorb, and energy supply while faced with a foe with infinite reinforcements.<br/>

<br/><div class="contentbox mw-contentbox ">
::'''<big>Victory window message</big>'''
::''Surprisingly, it is better to go for attackers first rather than to build the Sector Scanner as soon as possible.'' </div>



=='''Hints and solution'''==

===General hints and tips===

{|   class="wikitable mw-collapsible mw-collapsed"
| <strong>+ Show hints&nbsp;</strong>
|-
| You have very little absorb in this mission, so clearing attackers is a high priority.
|-
| The enemies in this mission come into play delayed, not under construction, so you may attack them as soon as you have breached all defenders.
|-
| As the victory window suggests, it is better to try to reach a stable position before constructing the Scanner than it is to get in an early scan or two.
|}
