= Frostbite =

{{Unit infobox}}

'''{{PAGENAME}}''' is a [[{{#var:type}} unit|{{#var:type}}]] [[{{#ifeq:{{lc:{{#var:spell}} }}|yes|spell|unit}}]]. It's an inexpensive source of {{ticon|chill}}[[chill]], but it [[self-sacrifice|self-sacrifices]] at use.

{{Unit panel}}

==Strategy==
{{PAGENAME}} can be considered the {{ticon|red}}[[red]] equivalent of [[Gauss Charge]] and [[Pixie]], in that it can be a sink for {{ticon|red}} that would otherwise have gone unused at the end of the turn.

At {{ticon|chill|3}}[[Chill]], {{PAGENAME}} is able to chill all [[blocker|defenders]] in the basic set, most notably [[Wall]].  Against [[breach]]-vulnerable units such as [[Tarsier]] and [[Shadowfang]], {{PAGENAME}}s can be stockpiled to threaten a [[breach]], forcing an opponent to spend an additional {{ticon|gold|5}}{{ticon|blue}} for Walls to defend against each {{ticon|gold|2}}{{ticon|red}} {{PAGENAME}}. A good counterplay to this is to build semi-breach-resistant units such as [[Drake]] and constantly allow small breaches (often for 0 or 1) since spending a large pile of {{PAGENAME}}s is a high cost to pay for only a small breach. Naturally, this counter is less effective if you have more vulnerable units such as [[Tarsier]], but still applies. The only time it really doesn't apply at all is in games featuring legendary absorbers such as [[Centurion]] or [[Defense Grid]], as in those games a breach for exact is fatal even if your opponent payed a high cost for it. Therefore Frostbite is usually very strong in games with legendary absorbers.

Frostbite is countered hard by vigilant defense units such as [[Xeno Guardian]] and [[Ossified Drone]].

Since {{PAGENAME}} chills for a fixed {{ticon|chill|3}}, having defenders with amounts of [[health]] that are not multiples of 3 ([[Forcefield]]s and [[Infusion Grid]]s, for example) can allow a player to defend more comfortably. In this scenario, since not every {{PAGENAME}} is able to chill for the maximum amount, the defending player needs less total defense to avoid getting breached. On the other hand, {{PAGENAME}}s can be paired with other chill that may be in the random set, especially [[Cryo Ray]], to chill for exact amounts and apply more pressure.

{{PAGENAME}} is also produced by both [[Frost Brooder]] and [[Endotherm Kit]].
