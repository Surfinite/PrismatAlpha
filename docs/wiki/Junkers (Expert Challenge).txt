= Junkers (Expert Challenge) =

__NOTOC__ <!--suppresses table of contents for the headers!-->

Junkers is the very first mission of the [[Episode 1|first episode]] in Prismata. Complete all the missions of the [[List of episode 1 missions|episode]] to unlock the expert mission challenges. This mission is a puzzle of assigning the optimal amount of damage. The difficulty comes from the high uneven (and prime) Health values of the dumpsters and the numerous possibilities of progressing through the turns. Below are hints and the one solution. <br/> <br/>

=='''Solution'''==

{|   class="wikitable mw-collapsible mw-collapsed"
| <strong>+ Show specific hints&nbsp;</strong>
|-
| Don‘t kill both litterbugs right at the start.
|-
| You will need to kill the factory asap, on turn 6. 
|-
| You cannot always hit the perfect amount of damage in Prismata. Throughout this mission however, lose only 1 damage!
|}

|-
| Turn 5: deal X / 12 / 7  
|-

|-
| Turn 7: deal X / 0 / 21
|-
| Turn 8: kill all.
