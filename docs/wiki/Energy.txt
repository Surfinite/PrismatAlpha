= Energy =

{{ticon|energy}}'''Energy''' is a [[resource]] needed to purchase some [[unit]]s. It is also needed to power some [[ability|abilities]].

Currently, all [[unit]]s which cost energy produce resources - primarily the different types of [[drone]]s. Therefore, [[unit]]s which use energy to power their [[ability|abilities]] have an influence on a player's ability to increase their economy.

''Any energy not spent is lost at the end of a turn.''

==Energy generating units==
{{Manual unit panels|
Engineer
}}
Part of the [[basic set]], the Engineer is the only base set [[unit]] that generates {{ticon|energy}}energy. It generates 1{{ticon|energy}} at the start of every [[turn]]. No other unit, with the exception of [[Ebb Turbine]], generates {{ticon|energy}}Energy.
