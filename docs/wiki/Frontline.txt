= Frontline =

'''Frontline''' [[unit]]s, represented by the icon {{ticon|Frontline}}, can be directly attacked even when {{ticon|block}}[[blocker]]s are still providing defense. To attack a frontline unit, you must {{ticon|click}}click it before ending your turn. Frontline Units often have high stats (especially {{ticon|Health}}[[health]]) relative to their cost to compensate for the disadvantage of being vulnerable to direct attack. 

Some Frontline units may also possess the {{ticon|block}}blocker [[Unit mechanics|mechanism]].  {{ticon|Frontline}}Frontline {{ticon|Blocker}}Blockers can be used to defend as any other blocker, provided the opposing player cannot (or has not chosen to) attack and destroy them directly. All Frontline units are vulnerable to direct attack, regardless of whether or not they are currently blocking. Before the opponent is able to amass sufficient {{ticon|Attack}}[[attack]] to destroy them directly, Frontline Blockers can [[absorb]] a large amount of damage due to their high {{ticon|health}}[[health]].
