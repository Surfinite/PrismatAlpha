= The Invasion =

__NOTOC__ <!--suppresses table of contents for the headers!-->
The Invasion is the final mission in The Onslaught section of Challange Pack 3. It is the only mission in the pack with a difficulty rating of three skulls.

<br/><div class="contentbox mw-contentbox ">
::'''<big>Victory window message</big>'''
::'' Congratulations! This puzzle is a monster. Many Lunarch developers and testers took multiple hours to complete it. Well done.'' </div>



=='''Hints and solution'''==

===General hints and tips===

{|   class="wikitable mw-collapsible mw-collapsed"
| <strong>+ Show hints&nbsp;</strong>
|-
| It's impossible to keep their [[Zemora Voidbringer|Zemora]] from firing at all, but don't let it fire twice. Try to leave the enemy with 15 [[Green resource|Gaussite]] when you finish off their last [[Conduit|Conduit]]
|-
| It's possible to complete this mission without building a single [[Tarsier|Tarsier]] or [[Gauss Cannon|Gauss Cannon]]
|-
| Destroying the [[Gauss Fabricator|Gauss Fabricator]] quickly enough that it would be worthwhile would necessitate leaving the Conduits alive longer and allow the Zemora to fire twice.
|}
