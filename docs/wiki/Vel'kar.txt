'''Vel&#39;kar''' is a [[colorless unit|colorless]] [[unit]] which is not available to players. Vel'kar serves as the boss of the current Prismata campaign.

Each turn Vel'Kar creates one [[Shard]]. When Vel'Kar has six [[Shard|Shards]] on the board then he can consume the six [[Shard|Shards]] and construct an [[Evicerator]]. Having an [[Evicerator]] on the board will not make Vel'Kar stop his spawning of [[Shard]].

The player can delay the spawn of [[Evicerator]] by destructing [[Shard|Shards]].
