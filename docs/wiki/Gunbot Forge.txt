'''Gunbot Forge''' is a [[colorless unit|colorless]] [[unit]] which is not available to players. Gunbot Forge is created by [[Exostructor]] and is only present in the boss mission: Vel'Kar.

A Gunbot Forge produces one [[Gunbot]] each turn.
