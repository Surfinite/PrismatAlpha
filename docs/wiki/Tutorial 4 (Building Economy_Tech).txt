= Tutorial 4 (Building Economy/Tech) =

{{stub}}

'''Tutorial Tutorial 4 (Building Economy/Tech)''' is the fourth level in the Prismata campaign.

==Setup==

The setup is similar to that of a normal game. Both players begin with 2 [[Engineer]]s and 6 [[Drone]]s.

Instead of the base set, the available units include [[Drone]], [[Engineer]], [[Blastforge]], [[Wall]], [[Steelsplitter]], [[Omega Splitter]], [[Conduit]], [[Gauss Cannon]], [[Gauss Fabricator]], and [[Xeno Guardian]].
