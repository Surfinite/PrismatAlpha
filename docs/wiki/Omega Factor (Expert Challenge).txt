= Omega Factor (Expert Challenge) =

__NOTOC__ <!--suppresses table of contents for the headers!-->

Omega Factor is the fourth mission of the [[Episode 1|first episode]] in Prismata. Complete all the missions of the [[List of episode 1 missions|episode]] to unlock the expert mission challenges. This mission is a puzzle that includes economy timing and assigning the optimal amount of damage while defending. Below are hints and a solution. <div class="contentbox mw-contentbox ">
::'''<big>Victory window message</big>'''
::'' This challenge admits a solution in which only a single Engineer is constructed. Can you find it?'' </div>

=== One Engineer ===
The victory screen hints that the challenge can be cleared without constructing more than one Engineer.

This solution constructs 1 Engineer, 5 Omega Splitters, 0 Steelsplitters, and 23 Walls.
