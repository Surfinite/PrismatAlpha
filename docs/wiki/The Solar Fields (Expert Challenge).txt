= The Solar Fields (Expert Challenge) =

__NOTOC__ <!--suppresses table of contents for the headers!-->

The Solar Fields is the fifth mission of the [[Episode 1|first episode]] in Prismata. Complete all the missions of the [[List of episode 1 missions|episode]] to unlock the expert mission challenges. This mission is a time challenge, where the enemy will overwhelm you with numbers after several turns, if you cannot breach first. After the 4th Gunbot Forge becomes online on turn 15, it becomes almost impossible to survive. Below are hints and one opening that you can win easily from. <br/> <br/>

| You cannot win the mission with The Advocate damage alone, you also need [[Steelsplitter|Steelsplitters]].

| More may be possible, three to four Blastforges however are sufficient to succeed.
