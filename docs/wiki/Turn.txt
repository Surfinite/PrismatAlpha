= Turn =

{{stub}}

A game of [[Prismata]] is divided into '''turns''' that include the [[Action]], [[Breach]] and [[Defense]] phases.

In general, a Prismata game goes through the following cycle, ending once one player's [[unit]]s are destroyed:
* Player's turn
* Player may attack, initiating a damage or defense phase
* Opponent's turn
* Opponent may attack, initiating a damage or defense phase
* Repeat

During a turn, a player can purchase units or trigger click [[Ability|abilities]], usually revolving around one or both of these two overall goals:
* Keeping his or her defense above the opponent's [[attack]] value, limiting how many units are permanently damaged or destroyed.
* Raising his or her attack value up to or above the opponent's defense in order to inflict maximum damage to opponent units.
