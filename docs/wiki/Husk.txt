==Strategy==
'''{{PAGENAME}}''' is a flexible defender that costs the same amount of {{ticon|gold}}[[gold]] as [[Engineer]] for the same amount of [[defense]] but has {{ticon|prompt}}[[Prompt]].

While being strictly inferior to an Engineer when considering cost for {{ticon|health}}[[Health]], it is useful in [[random set]]s containing high cost {{ticon|red}} [[red unit]]s such as [[Lucina Spinos]] or [[Tatsu Nullifier]]: these units often require having 2 [[animus|Animus]] in play for a total of {{ticon|red}}4 produced every turn, most of which would usually get wasted as soon as you enter the mid/late game where your gold has to go towards buying defense. {{PAGENAME}} provides a sink for this otherwise wasted {{ticon|red}} and lets you improvise your defense more easily with its {{ticon|prompt}}Prompt.

{{PAGENAME}} is also great for improving your [[defensive granularity]] without delay if you realize your opponent might try to [[offensive finesse|finesse]] your defense next turn.
