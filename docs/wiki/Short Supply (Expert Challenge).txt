= Short Supply (Expert Challenge) =

__NOTOC__ <!--suppresses table of contents for the headers!-->

Short Supply is the 7th mission of the [[Episode 2|second episode]] in the [[Campaign|Campaign]]. The player starts this mission with most of their stock of units already depleted, and thus must achieve victory by utilizing a small number of a variety of units.<br/>

<br/><div class="contentbox mw-contentbox ">
::'''<big>Victory window message</big>'''
::'' For an extra challenge, try winning without depleting the supplies of more than 3 different units.  How low can you go?'' </div>
