= Gameplay changes =

{{stub}}
This page lists gameplay changes throughout Prismata's development. Sourced from [https://docs.google.com/document/d/1WiXto-YcNX8KT6xcBgYJTZgrjxprp26csePVltZN8g0 official changelog].

==May 1st, 2015==
*Added four new units: [[Corpus]], [[Nitrocybe]], [[Vai Mauronax]], [[Xaetron]].

==April 25th, 2015==
*[[Cluster Bolt|Cluster Bolt's]] constructed units were decreased from 5 Gauss Charges for player and 2 for opponent to 4 for player and 1 for opponent.

==April 7th, 2015==
*[[The Wincer|The Wincer's]] cost increased from 8{{ticon|gold}}{{ticon|green}}{{ticon|blue}}{{ticon|red|2}} to 9{{ticon|gold}}{{ticon|green}}{{ticon|blue}}{{ticon|red|2}}.
*[[Redeemer|Redeemer's]] cost changed from 8{{ticon|gold}}{{ticon|green|2}}{{ticon|blue}} to 7{{ticon|gold}}{{ticon|green|3}}{{ticon|blue}}.

==April 2nd, 2015==
*Added a new unit: [[Resophore]].

==March 22nd, 2015==
*Added four new units: [[Ebb Turbine]], [[Kinetic Driver]], [[Nivo Charge]], [[The Wincer]].

==February 19th, 2015==
*[[Auric Impulse|Auric Impulse's]] cost decreased from 4{{ticon|gold}}{{ticon|energy}} to 3{{ticon|gold}}{{ticon|energy}}.
*Auric Impulse's produced resources decreased from 5{{ticon|gold}}{{ticon|energy}} to 4{{ticon|gold}}.
*Added three new units: [[Arka Sodara]], [[Mahar Rectifier]], [[Redeemer]].

==February 15th, 2015==
*[[Ossified Drone|Ossified Drone's]] cost changed from 5{{ticon|gold}}{{ticon|red}}{{ticon|energy}} to 2{{ticon|gold}}{{ticon|red}} + sacrifice a Drone.
*[[Grenade Mech]] now has a click ability: Consume a Blastforge to Construct 2 Pixies.
*[[Asteri Cannon|Asteri Cannon's]] cost increased from 16{{ticon|gold}}{{ticon|green|3}} to 16{{ticon|gold}}{{ticon|green|4}}.
*Asteri Cannon's health increased from {{ticon|fragile}}9 to {{ticon|fragile}}11.
*Asteri Cannon's ability's activation cost increased from {{ticon|fragile}}4 to {{ticon|fragile}}5.
*[[Steelforge|Steelforge's]] cost decreased from 3{{ticon|gold}} + sacrifice a Blastforge to 2{{ticon|gold}} + sacrifice a Blastforge.
*[[Lucina Spinos|Lucina Spinos']] cost decreased from 18{{ticon|gold}}{{ticon|red|4}} to 17{{ticon|gold}}{{ticon|red|4}}.
*[[Frost Brooder|Frost Brooder's]] lifespan decreased from {{ticon|lifespan}}9 to {{ticon|lifespan}}7.
*[[Deadeye Operative|Deadeye Operative's]] cost increased from 8{{ticon|gold}}{{ticon|blue|2}} to 11{{ticon|gold}}{{ticon|blue|2}}.
*Deadeye Operative attack increased from {{ticon|attack}}1 to {{ticon|attack}}2.
*Deadeye Operative's ability's activation cost increased from {{ticon|attack}}1 to {{ticon|attack}}2.
*Deadeye Operative's health increased from {{ticon|health}}2 to {{ticon|health}}4.
*[[Tia Thurnax|Tia Thurnax's]] cost increased from 4{{ticon|gold}}{{ticon|green|3}}{{ticon|red}} + sacrifice 7 Drones to 6{{ticon|gold}}{{ticon|green|3}}{{ticon|red}} + sacrifice 7 Drones.
*[[Scorchilla|Scorchilla's]] cost decreased from 7{{ticon|gold}}{{ticon|green}}{{ticon|red}} to 5{{ticon|gold}}{{ticon|green}}{{ticon|red}}.
*Scorchilla's build time increased from {{ticon|buildtime}}1 to {{ticon|buildtime}}2.
*[[Endotherm Kit|Endotherm Kit's]] cost increased from 4{{ticon|gold}}{{ticon|green|2}}{{ticon|red|2}} to 5{{ticon|gold}}{{ticon|green|2}}{{ticon|red|2}}.
*Endotherm Kit's supply decreased from 4 to 1.
*Endotherm Kit's build time increased from {{ticon|buildtime}}1 to {{ticon|buildtime}}4.
*Endotherm Kit's constructed units increased from 2 Frostbites + 2 Cryo Rays to 4 Frostbites + 4 Cryo Rays.
*[[Auric Impulse|Auric Impulse's]] cost changed from 5{{ticon|gold}} to 4{{ticon|gold}}{{ticon|energy}}.
*Auric Impulse's produced resources changed from 6{{ticon|gold}} to 5{{ticon|gold}}{{ticon|energy}}.

==January 8th, 2015==
* [[Vivid Drone|Vivid Drone's]] cost increased from 4{{ticon|gold}}{{ticon|energy|3}} to 4{{ticon|gold}}{{ticon|energy|3}} + sacrifice 3 Drones.
* Vivid Drone gold production increased from 2{{ticon|gold}} to 5{{ticon|gold}}.
* Vivid Drone gold production changed from click-action to start-action.
* Vivid Drone is no longer a blocker.
* [[Cryo Ray|Cryo Ray's]] health increased from {{ticon|fragile}}2 to {{ticon|fragile}}3.
* Cryo Ray's ability now costs {{ticon|fragile}}1 health to activate.
* [[Doomed Drone|Doomed Drone's]] lifespan increased from {{ticon|lifespan}}3 to {{ticon|lifespan}}4.
* Added two new units: [[Endotherm Kit]], [[Infusion Grid]].

* [[Drake|Drake's]] cost increased from 15{{ticon|gold}}{{ticon|blue|3}} to 16{{ticon|gold}}{{ticon|blue|3}}.
* [[Ossified Drone|Ossified Drone's]] cost decreased from 6{{ticon|gold}}{{ticon|energy}}{{ticon|red}} to 5{{ticon|gold}}{{ticon|energy}}{{ticon|red}}.
* [[Deadeye Operative|Deadeye Operative's]] cost increased from 7{{ticon|gold}}{{ticon|blue|2}} to 8{{ticon|gold}}{{ticon|blue|2}}.
* Deadeye Operative is no longer a blocker.
* Deadeye Operative attack increased from {{ticon|attack}}0 to {{ticon|attack}}1.
