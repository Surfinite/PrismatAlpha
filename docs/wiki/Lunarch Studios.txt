= Lunarch Studios =

{{stub}}
Conceived in 2010, '''Lunarch Studios''' is a small Canadian-controlled gaming startup based in Waterloo, Ontario, Canada. Currently focused on free-to-play strategy games, one of their first offerings is [[Prismata]], a hybrid-genre game borrowing elements from collectible card and real-time strategy games.
