= Tandem (Expert Challenge) =

__NOTOC__ <!--suppresses table of contents for the headers!-->

Tandem is the 2nd mission of the [[Episode 2|second episode]] in the [[Campaign|Campaign]]. The mission is a test of endurance where the player attempts to hold the attacking forces at bay while Anya builds up enough [[Tarsier|Tarsiers]] and [[Shadowfang|Shadowfangs]] to defeat the enemy forces.<br/>
