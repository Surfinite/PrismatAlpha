= Blocker =

'''Blockers''', represented by the icon {{ticon|blocker}}, are units that can add their current [[health]] to a player's [[defense]] for each turn. Note that blockers cannot do this when they use their {{ticon|click}}[[ability]] during a turn. This often requires making tradeoffs between inflicting [[attack]] and maintaining adequate defense.

Blockers that are [[prompt]] can add defense value during the same turn they are purchased due to their zero [[build time]].
Blockers with [[Lifespan]] are [[lifespan blocker counters|vulnerable in certain situations]].
