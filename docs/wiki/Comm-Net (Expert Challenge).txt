= Comm-Net (Expert Challenge) =

__NOTOC__ <!--suppresses table of contents for the headers!-->

Comm-Net is the eighth mission of the [[Episode 1|first episode]] in Prismata. Complete all the missions of the [[List of episode 1 missions|episode]] to unlock the expert mission challenges. This mission is a an aggressive survival puzzle, where the enemy will overwhelm with numbers after several turns, if you do not succeed to do it first. Unlike similar [[VILE (Expert Challenge)|previous missions]], [[Supply|supply]] is added: As in regular play, a maximum number of the same type of unit can be build. For [[Wall|Walls]] that is a max of 10, so starting to deal damage early on is important. Below are hints and two winning builds. <br/>

=='''Hints and solution'''==

''Info: Puzzles may have more than one successful strategy and solution. The two lines described (progressively) below either open with a strong economy, or with very early damage.  
{|   class="wikitable mw-collapsible mw-collapsed"
| <strong>+ Show general hints&nbsp;</strong>
|-
| [[Shadowfang|Shadowfangs]] are much more immediate attackers than [[Tarsier|Tarsiers]], which in turn are much more cost-efficient than [[Steelsplitter|Steelsplitters]].
|-
| There is no need to try adding drones after the starting phase in this mission. 
|-
| You should be breaching the enemy defences (prioritize the non-tough Gunbots at first) around the same time as defending becomes almost impossible. Keep your [[Overteching|tech building]] number as low as feasible.
|-
| It's ok to lose a lot, but not all, [[Drone|Drones]] on defence, to keep having the ideal [[Absorb]absorb].
|}
