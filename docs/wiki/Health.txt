= Health =

{{stub}}
A [[unit]]'s {{ticon|health}}'''health''' is the amount of {{ticon|attack}}[[attack]] required to destroy a unit. It is also the amount of {{ticon|chill}}[[Chill]] required to prevent a unit from {{ticon|block}}[[defense|defending]].

Most units heal from any [[attack]] they survive between turns. {{ticon|fragile}}[[Fragile]] units are an exception as they do not heal. All damage they take permanently reduces their health.

Some green units, such as [[Asteri Cannon]] or [[Cryo Ray]], use their own health as a resource. [[Ability|Clicking]] these units provides an effect in exchange for paying some amount of health. As a result, these units may only be clicked a limited number of times.
