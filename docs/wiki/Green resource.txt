= Green resource =

{{ticon|green}} '''Green resource''' (also known as ''Gaussite'') is a crystalline [[resource]] needed to purchase some [[unit]]s. It is also needed to power some [[ability|abilities]].

[[Green unit]]s are high technology units typically involving forcefield, energy weapons, or other energy-based phenomena.

Like [[gold]] and unlike the other resources, unspent green resource is ''not'' lost between turns.

==Green Resource Generating Units==
{{Manual unit panels|
Conduit
}}
Part of the [[basic set]], the conduit is the most common unit that generates green resource. It generates one at the start of every turn.
