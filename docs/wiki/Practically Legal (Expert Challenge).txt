= Practically Legal (Expert Challenge) =

__NOTOC__ <!--suppresses table of contents for the headers!-->

Practically Legal is the 1st mission of the [[Episode 3|third episode]] in the [[Campaign|Campaign]]. The mission presents the player with waves of enemies to fight off with a powerful attacking unit with intermittent availability.<br/>

<br/><div class="contentbox mw-contentbox ">
::'''<big>Victory window message</big>'''
::''Wave-clearing missions like these have a strong "snowball effect". The first few turns are extremely important!'' </div>



=='''Hints and solution'''==

===General hints and tips===

{|   class="wikitable mw-collapsible mw-collapsed"
| <strong>+ Show hints&nbsp;</strong>
|-
| You have very little absorb in this mission, so clearing attackers is a high priority.
|-
| The enemies in this mission come into play delayed, not under construction, so you may attack them as soon as you have breached all defenders.
|-
| Obviously you will want to get 4 Conduits as quickly as you can.
|-
| Attacking with Rhino is more efficient than attacking with Perforator.
|-
| It is usually better to absorb on a prompt Rhino than on a Perforator which isn't clicked, as holding perforators is not cost effective; perforators should instead be used as defenders.
|}

===Solution===
The [[Photonic Rainmaker]] should be clicked on any turn where you have 13 or more Gaussite.
