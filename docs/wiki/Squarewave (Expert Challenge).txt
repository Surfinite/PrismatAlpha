= Squarewave (Expert Challenge) =

__NOTOC__<!--suppresses table of contents for the headers!-->

Squarewave is the second mission of the [[Episode 1|Episode 1: Outbreak]] in [[Prismata]]. The expert challenge is unlocked by clearing all missions in [[List of episode 1 missions|Episode 1]]. This mission has two parts. In the first part, all enemy units must be destroyed by turn 11. The enemy can't attack, but the time limit means only an efficient attack will win. In the second part, the enemy quickly builds Steelsplitters to attack the player.<br/> <br/>
