= Cost =

The '''cost''' of a [[unit]] or {{ticon|click}}[[ability]] is an action that must be taken in order to purchase or activate said unit or ability.

==Unit costs==
All units have costs related to purchasing them. This cost will always include at least one [[resource]] and may include sacrificing one or multiple [[unit]]s. This is often one of the more common uses of resources during games, due to these costs generally being higher and having more lasting impact.

Unit costs are often listed by giving the number of gold required followed by one letter ([[Red|R]], [[Green|G]], [[Blue|B]], [[Energy|E]]) for each required resource. For example [[Centurion]]'s cost can be typed as 18GGBBR. In this Wiki resource symbols are used instead (e.g. 18{{ticon|gold}}{{ticon|green|2}}{{ticon|blue|2}}{{ticon|red}}).

==Ability costs==
Unit [[ability|abilities]] may have costs related to them. This cost may include [[resource]]s, sacrificing [[unit]]s or losing [[health]] in the case of {{ticon|fragile}}[[Fragile]] units. Generally, only units with {{ticon|blocker}}[[Blocker]] will have abilities without costs associated with them, as the inability to block after being clicked it itself a cost.
