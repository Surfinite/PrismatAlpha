= Stamina =

[[Unit]]s with limited '''stamina''', represented by the icon {{ticon|Stamina}}, can only use their {{ticon|click}}[[ability]] a limited number of times. Since they have to be {{ticon|click}}clicked, they provide [[offensive finesse]].  Afterwards, these units are typically only useful as [[blocker]]s.
