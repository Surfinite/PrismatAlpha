= Build time =

{{stub}}
A [[unit]]'s '''build time''', signified by the icon {{ticon|buildtime}}, is the number of [[turn]]s after purchase before the unit is actually active on the field.

[[Prompt]] [[blocker]]s are units that have zero build time, but note that even these units cannot use their [[ability]] in the same turn they are purchased.
[[Category:Unit mechanics]]

A unit is shown to be currently building with a yellowed tile and is said to be '''Under Construction'''. Units that are '''Under Construction''' do not affect the boardstate, and can't be attacked unless ''all'' of the owner's units are not under construction.
