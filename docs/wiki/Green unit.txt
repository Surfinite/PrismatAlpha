= Green unit =

'''{{PAGENAME}}s''' are those that cost only {{ticon|green}}[[green]] [[technology]].

Green units tend to have a high amount of {{ticon|hp}}[[Fragile]] [[health]] for their {{ticon|gold}}[[gold]] cost, with the downside of providing no [[absorb]] or {{ticon|lifespan}}[[Lifespan|lifespan]] (except for the [[Fission Turret]]). 
There are three kind of green [[attack|attacker]]s:
;Low-cost
:This set contains units like [[Gauss Charge]], [[Fission Turret]] and [[Cryo Ray]]. These units are rather cheap as they only require some {{ticon|gold}}[[gold]] and one {{ticon|green}}[[green]]. They can be spammed, however they do not last long.
;Medium-cost
:Units like [[Tantalum Ray]] and [[Iso Kronus]] have moderate cost, respectively {{ticon|gold|7}}{{ticon|green|2}} and {{ticon|gold|5}}{{ticon|green|1}}. They are rather efficient and provide good [[breach]] potential.
;Specific units
:These units like [[Zemora Voidbringer]] and [[Asteri Cannon]] have a high cost in {{ticon|gold}}[[gold]] and/or {{ticon|green}}[[green]] but hold high value in [[Random set]] where they can not be countered. They have high {{ticon|hp}}[[fragile]] and some also have an useful {{ticon|click}}[[Ability]].

Green units also count great [[blocker|defensive units]]. These units do have different use-cases:
*[[Plexo Cell]] and [[Barrier]] have a [[Lifespan]] of {{ticon|lifespan|1}}. Barrier is used largely in place of Forcefield to avoid sacrificing [[Drone]]s unnecessarily. Plexo Cell can block huge bursts of {{ticon|attack}}[[attack]] efficiently and is one of the best [[blocker|defender]]s in the game provided there is no {{ticon|chill}}[[chill]].
*[[Aegis]] and [[Forcefield]] do not have limited [[Lifespan]] so they have a higher cost however they are still [[Fragile|Fragile unit]]. They provide efficient health for [[defense]] but no [[absorb]].
