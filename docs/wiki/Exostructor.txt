'''Exostructor''' is a [[colorless unit|colorless]] [[unit]] which is not available to players. It is used by [[Vel'kar]] to produce offensive units. Exostructor are present only in boss mission: Vel'kar.

Exostructor creates unit each turn from turn 4 (or 2 in hard mode). The construct order is first a [[Gunbot]], then a [[Tough Gunbot]] and finally a [[Gunbot Forge]] then looping back to a [[Gunbot]].
