==Strategy==
Although Galvani Drone is a frontline unit, killing it is not usually correct unless it denies absorb. If you always kill them (and you never deny absorb), then you are essentially letting the opponent buy preemptive defense for only a small fraction of a gold per HP, because the Galvani Drone costs 1 gold now, but also returns that gold on the next turn before it dies.

It is almost always correct to buy at least one or two Galvani Drones and replace the Engineers powering them if you can tell you are going to lose them, as in most games you will want to be buying some engineers in the mid- and late-game anyway.

[[Sentinel]] makes a strong combo with {{PAGENAME}} because each Sentinel can basically keep one Engineer on your board without you having to specifically buy them. This allows you to support many more {{PAGENAME}}s than you could without them.

[[Antima Comet]] is an incredibly strong synergy. See the page for details.
