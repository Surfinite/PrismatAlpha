= Synthesizer =

{{Unit infobox}}

'''{{PAGENAME}}''' is a [[technology|technological]] [[{{#var:type}} unit|{{#var:type}}]] [[{{#ifeq:{{lc:{{#var:spell}} }}|yes|spell|unit}}]]. It produces {{ticon|green}}[[green]] and can transform {{ticon|green}} into {{ticon|blue}}[[blue]].

==Panel==
{{Unit panel}}

==Strategy==
A Synthesizer can transform {{ticon|green|3}}[[green]] into {{ticon|blue|2}} if you use its {{ticon|click}}[[Ability]]. It allows you to construct [[Blue unit|blue units]] that cost {{ticon|blue|3}} with a single [[Blastforge]]. 

It is often a good purchase in [[random set]]s with strong [[green units]] as [[Zemora]] or [[Cluster bolt]], or large [[blue unit]] such as [[Odin]] or [[Defense Grid]], as buying them normally off triple [[Blastforge]] would result in wasting {{ticon|blue}}. An exception to this is the [[Drake]] as it needs a [[Blastforge]] to use its {{ticon|click}}[[Ability]] in order to maximize its [[threat]].

Note that Synthesizer is slightly more efficient for producing green than Conduits, but slightly less efficient at producing blue than Blastforges.
