= Emote =

'''Emotes''' are displayable phrases and icons shown by clicking ones avatar during a match and selecting the desired emote. There are 9 slots for emotes that show up by clicking ones avatar, and emotes for these slots can be changed through the emote tab. You can also search in-game for any emote that you own. Most emotes can be won in the [[Armory]], but certain emotes are only available to different kickstarter tiers or with the use of a promo code.
