= Gold =

{{ticon|Gold}}'''Gold''' is the primary [[resource]] used to purchase [[unit]]s each [[turn]]. Nearly every unit in the game has a {{ticon|Gold}}gold cost.

''Any Gold not spent is stored at the end of a turn.''

==Gold generating units==
The primary means of generating {{ticon|Gold}}gold is through various types of units - primarily drones.

===The basic drone===
{{Manual unit panels|
Drone
}}
Part of the [[basic set]], this drone is the primary means of generating {{ticon|Gold}}gold. Each [[turn]], it generates {{ticon|Gold|1}} by using its {{ticon|click}}[[ability|click ability]].
