==Strategy==
'''Aegis''' is a {{ticon|fragile}}[[fragile]] {{ticon|prompt}}[[prompt]] {{ticon|block}}[[blocker|defensive]] unit with heavy cost in {{ticon|green}}[[green]]. It is mostly useful in the mid and late game as it does not [[absorb]], so it should be bought only if your opponent must deal more than {{ticon|attack}}5 constant [[attack]].

Aegis is an extremely efficient defender when compared to blue units that absorb (costs less than [[Energy Matrix]] while providing the same HP). However, it requires many Conduits for it to be bought every turn.

Aegis is vulnerable to being exploited by [[offensive finesse]] due to its high HP.

Unlike [[Forcefield]] and [[Plexo Cell]], Aegis does not consume any [[drone]]s, so it doesn't hurt your economy.
