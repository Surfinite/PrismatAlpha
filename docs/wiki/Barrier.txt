==Panel==
{{Unit panel}}

==Strategy==
Being {{ticon|prompt}}[[Prompt]] and costing very little, Barrier is useful as a small defender for situations where you only need 1 more defense and thus buying a [[Wall]] or [[Aegis]] would be wasteful. Buying several Barriers is usually preferable to buying a smaller number of [[Forcefield]]s since the Barriers do not require [[Drone]] sacrifices; however, holding a Drone and buying a Barrier is far worse than buying a Forcefield (it's essentially the same thing except it costs {{ticon|gold|1}} more). As Barrier is not particularly efficient soak even by base set standards (Wall is slightly better), it should not be your main source of defense, but should instead act mostly as a [[tech sink|green sink]].

Also, like other blockers with Lifespan, Barrier has [[lifespan blocker counters|counters]].
