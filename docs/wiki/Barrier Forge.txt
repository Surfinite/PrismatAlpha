'''Barrier Forge''' is a [[colorless unit|colorless]] [[unit]] which is not available to players. Barrier Forge is created by [[Endostructor]] and is only present in the boss mission: Vel'Kar.

A Barrier Forge produces one [[Weak Barrier]] each turn.
