= Exhaust =

'''Exhaust''', represented by the icon {{ticon|Exhaust}}, can refer to either:
* The number of turns a [[unit]]'s start-of-turn effect is disabled after it triggers.
* The number of turns a unit's click [[ability]] is disabled after that ability is used.

In addition to disabling the unit's start-of-turn and click abilities, exhaust also prevents units from being consumed. Units that consume a unit as part of their purchase cost (i.e. [[Forcefield]]) or click ability (i.e. [[The Wincer|Wincer]]) cannot use exhausted units. However units that destroy a unit as part of their click ability (i.e. [[Deadeye Operative]]) can be used to destroy exhausted units. Similarly, exhausted units can be destroyed by damage.
