= Zoo (Expert Challenge) =

__NOTOC__ <!--suppresses table of contents for the headers!-->

Zoo is the 6th mission of the [[Episode 2|second episode]] in the [[Campaign|Campaign]]. The player faces a relentless onslaught of Red units with a limited set of Red units to use themselves to fend off the oncoming horde.<br/>

=='''Hints and solution'''==

===General hints and tips===

{|   class="wikitable mw-collapsible mw-collapsed"
| <strong>+ Show hints&nbsp;</strong>
|-
| The first phase of this mission, before Swade shows up, is easier to manage with a large offense fueled by [[Shadowfang|Shadowfangs]] than by trying to build up defenses that can absorb the incoming damage.
|-
| It is easier to defend with [[Engineer|Engineers]] or [[Perforator|Perforators]] than with any other unit in your deck for this mission.
|-
| There is at least one solution which buys no additional [[Animus]].
|}
