= Incendiary Biorobotics =

__NOTOC__ <!--suppresses table of contents for the headers!-->

Incendiary Biorobotics is the bonus mission and last (9th) mission of the [[Episode 1|first episode]] in the [[Campaign|Campaign]]. This mission is an endurance test, where the player must juggle defending against an ever-increasing amount of damage with destroying attackers before they overwhelm you.<br/>
