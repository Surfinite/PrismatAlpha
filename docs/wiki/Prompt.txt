= Prompt =

Units that are '''prompt''', represented by the icon {{ticon|prompt}}, are {{ticon|block}}[[blocker]]s that immediately add their {{ticon|health}}[[health]] to your {{ticon|block}}[[defense]] when purchased, making them useful for quickly shoring up inadequate defense. Note that prompt does not quite mean "zero {{ticon|buildtime}}[[build time]]", as prompt units cannot use any [[ability|abilities]] they may have on the turn they are purchased.
