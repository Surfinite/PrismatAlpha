= Lifespan =

Some [[units]] have a limited '''lifespan''', shown by a skull icon {{ticon|lifespan}}. At the start of the owner's [[turn]], the unit's lifespan is reduced by one, and it's destroyed when it reaches zero.

[[Blocker|Blockers]] with lifespan are vulnerable to [[chill]], since freezing a blocker that's about to die anyway is equivalent to dealing damage. Similarly, absorb denial units like [[Bloodrager]] and flexible attackers like [[Scorchilla]] can reduce a lifespan unit's value as a blocker. For these reasons, it's sometimes better to attack with a lifespan blocker on its last turn even if the defensive value would be greater.
