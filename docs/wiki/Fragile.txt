= Fragile =

'''Fragile''' [[unit]]s, represented by a heart icon {{ticon|Fragile}}, do not heal from [[attack]] they have survived. Instead, damage permanently reduces their [[health]], while other units recover full health by the next turn as long as they survive.

All Fragile Units cost [[Green resource|Green]] except Conduit and Thorium Dynamo, which are [[Colorless unit|colorless]].
