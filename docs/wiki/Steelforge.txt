==Strategy==

The '''{{PAGENAME}}''' is a colorless unit that requires the player to sacrifice a [[Blastforge]] to be built. Its {{ticon|click}}[[Ability]] allows you to efficiently convert your [[Drone]]s into [[Steelsplitter]]s for {{ticon|gold|1}}{{ticon|blue|2}} ({{ticon|gold}}[[gold]] and {{ticon|blue}}[[blue]]). As {{PAGENAME}} has {{ticon|health|4}} [[health]] it can not be targeted by the [[Apollo]]'s {{ticon|click}}[[Ability]] (or [[Kinetic Driver]]'s for that matter), so building {{PAGENAME}} can save your {{ticon|blue}}blue production from being sniped at a critical moment.

Buying {{PAGENAME}} instead of a second Blastforge is virtually always correct unless there are Blastforge-synergy units such as [[Drake]] or [[Grenade Mech]] as it's essentially just a 1-gold cheaper Blastforge even without its ability.

{{PAGENAME}}'s click ability is significantly more efficient than buying Steelsplitters directly, but still less efficient than most random set attackers, so buying multiple {{PAGENAME}}s just to mass produce Steelsplitters is not recommended. The ability mostly acts as a [[tech sink|blue sink]].

When you use the {{PAGENAME}}'s {{ticon|click}}Ability you should not buy new Drones to replace the converted ones as they won't have time to pay for themselves, so you should rather invest your [[Resource]]s to buy more {{ticon|attack}}attack.

Note that if the [[Random set]] contains more Drone consuming units like [[Plasmafier]], you should be careful about combining them as they may [[Drone consuming nombo|reduce your Drone count]] down fast.
