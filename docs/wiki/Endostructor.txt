'''Endostructor''' is a [[colorless unit|colorless]] [[unit]] which is not available to players. It is used by [[Vel'kar]] to produce defensive units. Endostructor are present only in this boss mission: Vel'kar.

Endostructor creates unit each turn from turn 4 (in normal mode) or 2 (in hard mode). The construct order is first a [[Weak Barrier]], then a [[Tough Barrier]] and finally a [[Barrier Forge]] then looping back to a [[Weak Barrier]].
