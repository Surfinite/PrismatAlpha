Community-derived Prismata unit stats and efficiency spreadsheets:

1. Unit efficiency calculations (attack values, defense values, overall efficiency)
   https://docs.google.com/spreadsheets/d/1MhZpWNFQDsCRcwFHxZOGCkozcrMYlECU7u1HLBObyOs/edit?gid=469944061#gid=469944061
   Notes from methodology tab:
   - R (resource base) = 1.40
   - 1 attack = 2 gold equivalent
   - 1 prompt defense = ~2.37 gold equivalent
   - Tarsier value = 7 gold
   - Def. Eff. = cost / prompt defense
   - Overall Eff. = cost * tarsier_value / total_value (smaller = better)
   - Breachproof Eff. = value (excluding health) / health
   - Delayed damage valued per R formula
   - Click abilities and energy largely ignored
